John Hyatt

I have to say we delayed making a decision on replacing our roof. These days, there are a NUMBER of shop front and 'virtual' roofing companies, and it is not always easy to separate the wheat from the chaff. Do NOT be fooled by Hassan's apparent youth! From the outset, he distinguished himself by keeping us completely in the loop. He was able to discuss options, quality, build type, and components without hesitation, and with complete certainty. He factored weather into the schedule, and was not afraid to shift dates to take maximum advantage of good weather days (I have seen TOO many neighbours' roofs completely uncovered while a drenching downpour thundered down on them!) During the build, Hassan's Crew was professional, courteous, and capable. They strove to keep the mess to a minimum, endeavoured to keep the neighbour's properties untouched, and cleaned the yard as much as possible (I have been watching the varying degrees of post-roofing cleanup - including the disaster that was my neighbour's!). Hassan maintained a watch on progress as the job continued, showing up at the end of day to assist in cleanup. He also checked in after the job to make sure we were completely happy with the job. This - unlike so many home-owning chores - was a pleasure!
